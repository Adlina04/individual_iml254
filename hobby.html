Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a mobile game with MOBA elements.
The game is played in 5v5 matches, with each team occupying one side of the map. There are three lanes connecting the bases, and each lane is defended by towers. minions spawn and march down the lanes
to the enemy base. Players control heroes with unique abilities, and work together with their team to take down towers, enemies and ultimately the enemy base.		 A player cannot attack the enemy base directly without taking down opposing turrets in at least one lane first. Each lane has three turrets that shoot at heroes and deal a ton of damage. To attack them
without taking damage, a player needs help from their team's minions. Minions constantly spawn at the teams' bases and travel down along each lane. The team to
successfully destroy all the turrets in one or all lanes will increase their chances of destroying the opposite team's base.		 Nickname: ℓιηαα.
